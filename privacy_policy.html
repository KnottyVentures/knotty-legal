2. What Data We Collect

Anonymous account identifier

When you first open the App, we automatically create an anonymous account through Firebase Authentication. This account is identified by a randomly generated user ID (UID). The UID is not linked to your real identity, e-mail, or any other personally identifying information.

Generated username

The App automatically generates a username for you (for example, "Knotty-AB12CD3"). You can change this username at any time. Your username is stored locally on your device and synchronized with our cloud backend so that other players can see it on the leaderboard.

Game statistics

We store your gameplay progress, including experience points (XP), player level, and accuracy percentage. This data is associated with your anonymous UID and is used to display the leaderboard.

Local-only data

Per-question statistics (which questions you have seen, how often you answered correctly) and your settings (sound, theme) are stored only on your device and are never sent to our servers.

3. What We Do Not Collect

To make our position absolutely clear, the App does not collect, request, or have access to:

  • Your real name, e-mail address, phone number, or postal address
  • Your location (GPS or otherwise)
  • Your camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or any other device sensors
  • Any device identifiers used for advertising (IDFA, AAID)
  • Any analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Any payment or financial information

The App does not contain advertisements and does not offer in-app purchases.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the data described in Section 2 only for the following purposes:

  • Providing the App's core functionality (saving your progress between sessions)
  • Displaying the global leaderboard so you can see your position relative to other players
  • Restoring your account if you reinstall the App on the same device

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to running the App.

5. Third Parties

We rely on the following third-party service to operate the App:

Firebase (Google LLC / Google Cloud)

We use Firebase Authentication to create your anonymous account and Firestore to store your game progress and the leaderboard. Firebase is operated by Google and your data is processed on Google's infrastructure.

  • Firebase Privacy and Security: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We have configured Firebase to collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the App.

We do not use any analytics platform (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or Mixpanel), advertising networks, or social media SDKs.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you keep the App installed and use it. Specifically:

  • Local data (per-question statistics, settings) remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data.
  • Cloud data (UID, username, XP, level, accuracy) is stored on Firebase until you delete your account.

You can delete your account at any time from within the App. When you delete your account, your cloud data is permanently removed from our servers within a reasonable period.

If you simply uninstall the App without first deleting your account, your cloud data remains on our servers indefinitely (associated with your anonymous UID, with no way for us to identify or contact you).

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access — You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — You may correct inaccurate data (for example, by changing your username in the App).
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — You may delete your account from within the App, which removes all associated cloud data.
  • Right to data portability — You may request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — You may object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — You may file a complaint with a supervisory authority. In the Czech Republic, this is the Office for Personal Data Protection (Úřad pro ochranu osobních údajů, www.uoou.cz).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at knottyventures@gmail.com. Because the App does not collect your real identity, you may need to provide us with your anonymous UID (visible in the App's settings) so we can locate your data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us at knottyventures@gmail.com and we will remove the data.

9. International Data Transfers

10. Security
